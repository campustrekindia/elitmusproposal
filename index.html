02 — Growth Strategies

7 ways to
10× the reach

Concrete, executable tactics ordered by impact — from content overhaul to community-led growth loops.

1

Reels-First Content Strategy

Instagram's algorithm heavily favours Reels. Create 15–30 second Reels: "Day in the life at Boeing", "How to crack a pH test", salary reveals, career tips. Target minimum 3 Reels per week. This single shift can multiply reach by 5–10×.

High Impact
2

Consistent Visual Brand System

Design 4–5 locked post templates (Job Alert, Success Story, Tip of the Week, Poll, BTS). Use a fixed color palette across all content. The feed should feel like a curated magazine, not a random bulletin board.

High Impact
3

User-Generated Content Engine

Ask every placed candidate to share a 30-second "I got hired!" video tagging @elitmus.official. Feature weekly as Reels or Stories. Each video reaches that person's entire network for free — viral social proof at zero cost.

High Impact
4

Educational Carousel Series

Post weekly swipeable carousels: "5 resume mistakes that got you rejected", "How eLitmus pH score compares to other tests", "Salary benchmarks for freshers 2026". Carousels drive the highest save rate of any format — triggering algorithmic reach.

High Impact
5

Weekly Posting Calendar

Mon: Job Alert · Wed: Educational carousel · Fri: Reel (success story or career tip) · Sat: Poll / Question Story. Consistency trains the algorithm to distribute every post to a larger audience automatically.

Medium Impact
6

Hashtag + Collab System

Use a tiered hashtag strategy (niche: #elitmus #pHtest → mid: #jobsforengineersindia → broad: #hiringnow). Run Instagram Collabs with partner companies — their followers see the post too, doubling reach at zero extra cost.

Medium Impact
7

Community Engagement Loop

Reply to every comment within 2 hours of posting (critical for the algorithm). Run monthly "Ask Me Anything" Stories with a placement officer. Feature follower questions in posts. Community drives loyalty, saves, and repeat visits.

04 — Execution Roadmap

90-Day
launch plan

A phased approach moving from foundations to full momentum — without overwhelming the team or requiring a big budget.

M1

📐 Month 1 — Foundation

  • Design full visual brand system — templates, color palette, typography
  • Refresh existing 9 post captions, hashtags and CTAs
  • Set up the 4-day weekly content calendar
  • Publish first 3 Reels in the success story format
  • Launch "UGC Ask" — reach out to placed candidates for video testimonials
M2

🚀 Month 2 — Momentum

  • Publish 3-part educational carousel series for freshers
  • Launch first Instagram Collab post with a hiring partner
  • Start weekly AMA Stories with eLitmus placement officer
  • Introduce interactive poll stickers and question-box Stories
  • Review analytics — double down on the top-performing content type
M3

📈 Month 3 — Scale

  • Run first UGC campaign: "Share your eLitmus story"
  • Pitch 2–3 micro-influencer collaborations in career/tech niche
  • Optimise bio and link-in-bio for maximum conversion rate
  • Deliver full month-3 performance report: follower growth, reach, saves
  • Set data-backed revised targets for months 4–6
